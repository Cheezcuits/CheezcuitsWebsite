About Me!

Hello, I'm Cheddar. You can also call me Cheezcuits or Keso or any variations of sorts. I enjoy playing video games, doing silly coding projects, and reading mangas and manhwas. I am currently a freshman at Polytechnic University of the Philippines or PUP for short.

What is this website all about?

This website revolves around me trying to hone my skills in programming languages, mainly focused on Web Development such as HTML, CSS, and possibly JavaScript. With the current state of the website (v1.2.3), it is still a work in progress.

This website is not meant for a professional manner, but more of a hobby/passion.

Expect changes to this website over time. It may be slow or not in a scheduled manner, but it will certainly improve, hopefully.

The background images are made by @JL_T4N on Twitter :>
